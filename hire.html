Hire WUTV

If you're looking for a student videographer or editor, you've come to the right place. WUTV videographers come equipped with professional equipment. We’d love to set you up with a student who can help capture your event!

In the past, WUTV members have filmed Ashoka’s Diwali, LNYF, and ALAS’s Carnival, as well as dozens of a cappella concerts and student theater productions. WUTV members have also helped produce promotional videos for groups on campus and many members are involved with university marketing departments.

Let us know about your project in the form below. We'll match you with an independent contractor who will contact you with a quote.
